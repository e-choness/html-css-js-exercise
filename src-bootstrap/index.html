Q&A

I became inspired to become a game developer due to my love for video games. Growing up, I spent countless hours playing games and being immersed in their worlds. The idea of being able to create my own interactive experiences and share them with others was incredibly appealing. I wanted to be a part of the process of crafting memorable and enjoyable experiences for players.

My favorite game genres include:

  • Role-playing games (RPGs)
    I enjoy the deep narratives, character development, and immersive worlds that RPGs offer. The ability to shape the story and explore vast and detailed environments is particularly captivating.
  • Action-adventure games
    This genre combines elements of exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat. I appreciate the balance between engaging gameplay mechanics and compelling storytelling found in many action-adventure games.
  • Puzzle games
    I find puzzle games to be mentally stimulating and satisfying. Solving challenging puzzles or unraveling intricate mysteries can be a rewarding experience.
  • Strategy games
    The strategic thinking and planning required in strategy games appeal to me. I enjoy the challenge of managing resources, making tactical decisions, and outsmarting opponents.

Here are some underrated or lesser-known games that I believe more people should play:

  • Undertale
    This indie RPG combines innovative gameplay mechanics, a unique story, and memorable characters. It challenges traditional RPG conventions and offers players multiple paths and endings based on their choices.
  • Inside
    This atmospheric puzzle-platformer delivers a haunting and thought-provoking experience. Its minimalist storytelling, beautiful art style, and clever puzzles create an engaging and mysterious world.
  • Hades
    This roguelike action RPG offers fast-paced combat, dynamic storytelling, and a captivating blend of Greek mythology and character-driven narratives. Its addictive gameplay loop and rewarding progression system make it highly enjoyable.
  • Ori and the Blind Forest
    A visually stunning metroidvania-style game with a heartfelt story, beautiful music, and challenging platforming. It provides a compelling emotional journey and a sense of wonder as players explore its enchanting world.
